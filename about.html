Our Mission

DevConf is dedicated to fostering innovation and collaboration within the developer community. We bring together thought leaders, practitioners, and enthusiasts to share knowledge and shape the future of software development.

History

Founded in 2018, DevConf has grown into one of the premier tech conferences, attracting developers from over 50 countries. Each year, we continue to raise the bar for technical content and community engagement.

What to Expect

  • 80+ Technical Sessions - From beginner-friendly to advanced deep dives
  • Networking Opportunities - Connect with peers and industry professionals
  • Hands-On Workshops - Learn by doing with expert instructors
  • Expo Floor - Discover cutting-edge tools and technologies
  • Social Events - Evening receptions and networking lounges
