Keynote Speakers

Sarah Chen

Chief Architect at CloudScale AI

Sarah has spent over 15 years building distributed systems and is currently leading the AI infrastructure team at CloudScale. She is a frequent contributor to several major open-source projects.

Her keynote will focus on the reality of implementing large language models in enterprise production environments.

Specialties:

Marcus Thorne

Senior Frontend Engineer at WebFlow

Marcus is a design-system enthusiast and a pioneer in CSS-in-JS patterns. He has helped scale frontend architectures for Fortune 500 companies.

In his session, Marcus will explore the upcoming features of CSS4 and how they will change the way we think about responsive design.

Specialties:

  • UI/UX Design
  • React & Next.js
  • Web Performance
